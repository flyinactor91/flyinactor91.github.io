    Bio
    I like learning the basics of things before moving on to the 'easy' way. This site, for example, is all hand-coded; I didn't want to learn using design software. If all you ever use is auto-pilot, you can't say you know how to fly a plane. A lot of what I've done started because I wanted to learn how to make it happen. I wanted to learn how you took a play from idea to closing night, so I produced and directed "The Bible: The Complete Word of God (Abridged)" which played in Jan 2013. This site exists because I wanted to know more about how a website works. It's simplistic, yes, but the foundation is there should I decide to make something bigger. One thing that comes from my love of learning anything and everything is that I am not specialized. I like to say that I am mediocre at everything. Some may not consider that to be a good quality, but I see it as versatility. Chances are, I will know enough to get the job done whatever the job may be, and if not, I know where to look to find out.
