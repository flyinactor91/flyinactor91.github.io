Michael duPont - mdupont.com

Hello. My name is Michael duPont. I'm a computer programmer, maker, theatre tech, and pilot based in Orlando, FL. I primarily work with embedded systems like the Raspberry Pi, Arduino, and Intel Edison, but I also have experience with a wide variety of languages, platforms, and skills. I'm one of those swing players that knows enough about a lot of things, preferring not to totally specialize; it is very helpful as a maker and prototyper. What I enjoy most is finding new and cool ways to combine technology, science, and theatre that further teaching and storytelling.
